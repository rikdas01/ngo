About us

Helping Hands raises resources to support non-profit organizations working in different thematic areas.Helping Hands is also an NGO and we look up to your generous support to cover our operational cost. Donate to help us continue on this movement to change lives while also availing a deduction under 80G of Income Tax Act. In case you intend to donate to multiple NGOs in India, who are also our partners, you can write to us at info@helpinghands.org.We assure to take full responsibility to rationally distribute the donation, for the projects and campaigns who are in most need.
